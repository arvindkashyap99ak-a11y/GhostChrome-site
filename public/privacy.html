GhostChrome Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

GhostChrome does not collect or store your browsing history, tabs, or page content.

What We Collect

  • If syncing is enabled: encrypted session blobs only (AES-GCM)
  • Email address for account login & subscription billing

What We Never Collect

  • No browsing history
  • No open tabs
  • No cookies
  • No clipboard data
  • No plaintext session info
  • No passwords

Encryption

All session data is encrypted client-side with AES-GCM. Your password and keys never leave your device.

Contact

For privacy requests email: privacy@ghostchrome.com
